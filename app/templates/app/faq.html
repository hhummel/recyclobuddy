Q: I signed up, but haven't gotten alerts. What's up with that?

A: We send a confirmation email when you sign up. Click on the link in that email. Also, make sure you clicked on email alerts or sms (text) alerts on the sign up page.

A: Simply sign up again with the settings you want. Use the same email and mobile number as you did previously. RecycloBuddy will replace your current alert.

Q: What do I do if the alerts tell me to put out the cans on the wrong day?
